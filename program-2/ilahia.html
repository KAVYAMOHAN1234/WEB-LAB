Empowering Future Innovators: Ilahia College of Engineering

Ilahia Trust has been at the forefront of educational excellence, establishing one of the first self-financing engineering colleges in Kerala. As a co-educational minority institution, Ilahia College of Engineering is approved by AICTE and proudly affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Kerala Technological University. We are committed to nurturing future innovators with a global perspective, providing quality education that keeps pace with the ever-evolving technological landscape.Established in 2002, the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering at ICET offers an undergraduate program (B.Tech.) in Electrical and Electronics Engineering. The department is committed to meeting the evolving demands of the industry by integrating emerging technologies into its curriculum and research initiatives.With a team of highly qualified, experienced, and dedicated faculty members, the department ensures a balanced emphasis on both theoretical knowledge and practical applications. Dynamism, Growth and Selfless Service

The motto behind the establishment of ICET is Dynamism, Growth and Selfless Service. It is the result of the vision of Ilahia Trust and its endeavor to equip the youth of our nation with the necessary technological and managerial skills to successfully face the global challenges.

Vision

To become a global technical institution of high repute by nurturing a spirit of academic excellence and pursuit of advanced technical research by imparting timeless core values to the learners to serve humankind.

Mission

To transform the learners into exceptional technocrats and entrepreneurs capable of meeting ever-changing challenges in the global society, by continually imparting high-quality outcome-based technical education.
